Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026 · Applies to sanjeevani-dental-clinic.vercel.app

The short version

This website is a simple information site. It has no user accounts, no cookies set by us, and no forms that store your data on our servers — because we have no servers storing data at all.

Appointment booking

When you fill the booking form, your details are turned into a WhatsApp message on your own phone and sent to our clinic number (+91 70053 78195) only when you press send inside WhatsApp. This website itself does not transmit, collect or store anything you type. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies to messages you send there.

Embedded services

We embed a Google Map so you can find the clinic, and load fonts and animation scripts from content-delivery networks (Google Fonts files are self-hosted; GSAP is loaded from Cloudflare's cdnjs). These third parties may see your IP address as part of serving those files, as with any website. The map only loads when you scroll to it.

Patient information at the clinic

Medical records created during your treatment at the clinic are kept confidential at the clinic and are never published or shared with third parties, except as required by law.

Reviews

Review quotes shown on this website are taken from our public Google Business Profile. To change or remove your Google review, use your Google account — reviews remain under your control, not ours.

Contact

About Us

About Sanjeevani Dental Clinic, Dimapur

Sanjeevani Dental Clinic is a women-owned dental clinic on Jain Temple Road, near Ram Janki School, in the heart of Dimapur. For more than 15 years we have cared for over 5,000 patients — from a child's first check-up to full smile restorations for grandparents.

Our name, Sanjeevani, comes from the legendary healing herb — and that is the promise we work by: treatment that heals, comforts and lasts. Dental surgeons, an oral surgeon and an orthodontist work together under one roof, so you never need to run around town for specialist care.

Our Promise

What We Stand For

Safety First

Every instrument is autoclave-sterilized between patients. Disposables are used once and discarded — no exceptions, no shortcuts.

Honest Advice

We explain what's wrong, what your options are and what each costs — in plain language, with a written estimate before treatment begins.

Gentle Care

Dental fear is real — we work slowly, explain each step and use modern anaesthesia so treatment stays comfortable for adults and children alike.

Modern Diagnosis

Technology That Shows You Everything

We diagnose with digital X-rays and intraoral cameras, then show you the images and explain them — so you see exactly what we see before deciding anything. Clear diagnosis means no unnecessary treatment.

  • Digital X-ray imaging with instant results
  • Intraoral camera examinations
  • Modern dental chairs and equipment
  • Strict autoclave sterilization protocol

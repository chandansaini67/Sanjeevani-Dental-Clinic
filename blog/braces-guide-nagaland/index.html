Braces in Nagaland: Types, Cost & Timeline

T
Dr. Tisso Written by Dr. Tisso (Orthodontist) · 5 July 2026

Thinking about straightening your teeth — or your child's — but confused by the options and prices? As Sanjeevani's orthodontist, here's the honest guide I give my own patients in Dimapur.

The three main options

Metal braces

The classic, and still the workhorse. Metal brackets and wires handle even complex movements and cost the least — ₹25,000–₹45,000 for a full treatment. Modern ones are smaller and more comfortable than the braces you remember.

Ceramic braces

Same system, but with tooth-coloured brackets that blend in — ₹35,000–₹60,000. Popular with working adults who want discretion without the cost of aligners.

Clear aligners

A series of nearly invisible, removable trays — ₹60,000–₹1,50,000 depending on how complex your case is. Great for mild-to-moderate correction and for people who value looks and convenience, provided you wear them 20–22 hours a day.

How long does it take?

Most treatments run 12 to 24 months. Minor crowding can be faster; big bite corrections take longer. At your first consultation I give you a realistic timeline for your teeth — not an average.

What about payment?

Braces are naturally paid in stages across your monthly adjustment visits, which makes the cost far more manageable than it first looks. We discuss this openly before you commit.

Which should you choose?

  • Budget and complex case: metal braces give the best result per rupee.
  • Want discretion, still affordable: ceramic braces.
  • Value near-invisibility and discipline isn't an issue: clear aligners.

Honestly, the "best" option is the one that fits your teeth, your life and your budget — which is exactly what a consultation is for. You can even drag a slider in our 3D Smile Studio to watch crooked teeth straighten.

Is my child the right age?

Around age 12–14 is common, once most adult teeth are in — but every child is different, and an early check by age 7 can spot issues worth watching. Adults, take heart: teeth move at any age, and a large share of my patients are grown-ups.

Frequently asked questions

How much do braces cost in Nagaland?

At Sanjeevani Dental Clinic in Dimapur: metal braces ₹25,000–₹45,000, ceramic ₹35,000–₹60,000, and clear aligners ₹60,000–₹1,50,000 for a full treatment, paid in stages across visits.

Which is better — braces or aligners?

Braces handle complex movements and cost less; aligners are nearly invisible and removable but need 20–22 hours daily wear. The best choice depends on your specific case, which an orthodontist assesses first.

How long will I wear braces?

Most treatments take 12–24 months. Minor corrections finish sooner; complex bite problems take longer. You get a realistic timeline for your own teeth at the first consultation.

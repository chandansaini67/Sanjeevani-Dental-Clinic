Root Canal Cost in Dimapur: A 2026 Guide

Dr. Deepika Jain Written by Dr. Deepika Jain (Dental Surgeon) · 5 July 2026

If a dentist has told you that you need a root canal, your very next question is probably: what will it cost? Here's an honest, no-jargon guide to root canal treatment (RCT) prices in Dimapur in 2026 — written by a dental surgeon, not a marketer.

The short answer

At Sanjeevani Dental Clinic, root canal treatment costs ₹3,000 to ₹6,000 per tooth. Front teeth sit at the lower end; back molars, which have more canals and are harder to reach, sit at the higher end. A protective crown afterwards is priced separately.

Root canal treatment in Dimapur typically costs ₹3,000–₹6,000 per tooth, plus a crown (₹1,500–₹12,000 by material) to protect the treated tooth.

What changes the price?

  • Which tooth it is. A front tooth usually has one canal; a molar can have three or four. More canals means more work, so more cost.
  • How infected it is. A straightforward case finishes in one sitting. A badly abscessed tooth may need two visits with medicine in between.
  • Whether you need a crown. Almost every back tooth needs a crown after RCT to stop it cracking. This is a separate, sensible cost — not an upsell.
  • Re-treatment. Redoing a failed old root canal is more complex than a fresh one.

Is a root canal cheaper than pulling the tooth?

Extraction costs less today — ₹1,500–₹3,000 versus ₹3,000–₹6,000. But an empty gap rarely stays harmless: neighbouring teeth drift, chewing weakens, and most people eventually replace the tooth with a bridge or an implant costing ₹25,000–₹45,000. Seen over a few years, saving your natural tooth with a root canal is almost always the cheaper path.

Why the range, not a fixed price?

An honest dentist can't quote an exact figure before seeing the tooth on an X-ray. The range tells you what to plan for; after examination you get a written estimate — and at our clinic, no treatment begins until you've seen and agreed to that number.

Does it have to hurt?

No. This is the biggest myth in dentistry. With modern local anaesthesia, the tooth is completely numb — most patients tell us it felt like a long filling. The pain people dread is the infection before treatment, which the root canal removes. You can even watch how it works in 3D in our Smile Studio.

How to keep the cost down

  • Don't delay. A small cavity fixed early may never need a root canal at all. Once infection reaches the nerve, RCT becomes the cheaper option versus losing the tooth.
  • Get the crown. Skipping the crown to save money often ends with a cracked tooth and a bigger bill.
  • Ask about the whole plan. We tell you the full cost — RCT plus crown — upfront, so there are no surprises mid-treatment.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a root canal cost in Dimapur?

₹3,000–₹6,000 per tooth at Sanjeevani Dental Clinic, with molars at the higher end. A protective crown afterwards is priced separately (₹1,500–₹12,000 by material).

Is a crown always needed after a root canal?

For back teeth, almost always — they take heavy chewing forces and can crack without a crown. Some front teeth can manage with a strong filling; your dentist advises per tooth.

Can a root canal be done in one visit?

Many can. Straightforward cases finish in a single sitting; heavily infected teeth may need two visits so medicine can work inside the tooth between them.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a dental check-up cost in Dimapur?

At Sanjeevani Dental Clinic a consultation and check-up costs ₹200–₹500, and it is usually adjusted against your treatment cost if you proceed the same visit.

Is root canal treatment painful?

No — with modern anaesthesia, most patients feel little to nothing during RCT. Many of our root canals finish in a single sitting. Read more on our root canal page.

Do you treat children?

Yes — kids are a big part of our family. We take extra time so children feel safe and comfortable. See kids' dentistry.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Walk-ins are welcome, but booking on WhatsApp guarantees your slot and avoids waiting. For emergencies, call +91 70053 78195 — we keep same-day slots for pain cases.

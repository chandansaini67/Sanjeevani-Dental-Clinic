Questions about costs

Why do you show price ranges instead of fixed prices?

Because honest dentistry can't quote a fixed price before seeing the tooth — a small cavity and a deep one need different work. The range tells you what to expect; the written estimate after examination tells you exactly.

Do I pay everything at once?

No. Multi-visit treatments like braces, crowns and implants are naturally paid in stages as the work progresses.

Is the consultation fee wasted if I don't proceed?

The ₹200–₹500 consultation includes examination and honest advice you keep either way — and if you do proceed, it's usually adjusted against your treatment cost.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction in Dimapur

Wisdom tooth extraction removes a painful, decayed or impacted third molar that has no healthy way to grow. At Sanjeevani Dental Clinic in Dimapur, extractions are done by our oral surgeon Dr. Anovili Chishi — ₹1,500–₹3,000 for simple removals and ₹3,000–₹6,000 for surgical or impacted cases.

When a wisdom tooth should go

Repeated pain or swelling at the back of the jaw
The tooth is impacted (stuck sideways in bone)
Deep decay too far back to fill properly
Food constantly trapping and gum infection
It's damaging the tooth next to it
Crowding pressure on other teeth

What happens, step by step

  1. X-ray & plan

    A digital X-ray shows the roots and nerve position, so the surgeon knows exactly what she's dealing with.

  2. Numbing

    Local anaesthesia makes the whole area numb — you feel pushing and pressure, never sharp pain.

  3. Removal

    Simple teeth come out in minutes; impacted ones are sectioned and removed carefully through a small opening.

  4. Stitches & instructions

    Dissolving stitches if needed, gauze pressure, and clear written aftercare instructions before you leave.

Aftercare

  • Bite on gauze for 45–60 minutes after the procedure
  • Cold compress outside the cheek for swelling, day one
  • No spitting, straws or smoking for 72 hours (protects the clot)
  • Soft, cool foods for 2–3 days; avoid the extraction side

Common questions

Is wisdom tooth removal painful?

The procedure itself is numb and painless. Expect soreness and some swelling for 2–4 days afterwards — well controlled with prescribed painkillers and cold compresses.

Do all wisdom teeth need removal?

No. If a wisdom tooth grows straight, bites properly and can be cleaned, it stays. We only advise removal when it is causing or will cause real trouble.

What is a dry socket and how do I avoid it?

It's when the healing clot dislodges, exposing bone — painful but preventable: no straws, spitting or smoking for three days, and follow the aftercare sheet we give you.

Can I go to work the next day?

After a simple extraction, usually yes. After a surgical one, plan an easy day or two — most patients are fully comfortable within a week.

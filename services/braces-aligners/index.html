Braces & Clear Aligners in Dimapur

Braces and clear aligners gradually move crooked or crowded teeth into a healthy, straight smile. At Sanjeevani Dental Clinic in Dimapur, orthodontic treatment is led by our orthodontist Dr. Tisso and costs ₹25,000–₹1,50,000 for a full treatment depending on the type you choose.

Braces can fix

  • Crooked or crowded teeth
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Teeth that stick out
  • Bite problems (overbite, underbite, crossbite)
  • Difficulty cleaning overlapping teeth
  • Smile confidence — at any age

What happens, step by step

  1. Orthodontic consultation

    Dr. Tisso examines your bite, takes records, and explains realistically what result to expect and how long it will take.

  2. Choose your type

    Metal (strongest value), ceramic (less visible) or clear aligners (nearly invisible, removable) — honest pros and cons of each.

  3. Fitting

    Brackets are bonded or your first aligner set is delivered. Adjustment visits follow every few weeks.

  4. Retention

    After the smile is straight, retainers keep it that way. Skipping this step is how teeth drift back — we won't let that happen.

Aftercare

  • Some pressure for 2–3 days after each adjustment is normal
  • Avoid very sticky and very hard foods with braces
  • Clean around brackets carefully — we teach you the technique
  • Wear aligners 20–22 hours a day for on-schedule results

Common questions

How long will I need braces?

Most cases take 12–24 months. Minor corrections can be faster; complex bites take longer. You'll get a realistic timeline at the first consultation — not a sales pitch.

Am I too old for braces?

No. Teeth move at any age — a large share of our orthodontic patients are working adults. Aligners make treatment nearly invisible if that's a concern.

Braces or aligners — which is better?

Braces handle complex movements and cost less; aligners are nearly invisible and removable but need discipline. Dr. Tisso will tell you honestly which suits your case.

Do braces hurt?

Fitting doesn't hurt. Expect a dull pressure for a few days after each adjustment — patients describe it as 'annoying, not painful'. Soft food and it passes.

Can I see how teeth straighten?

Yes — drag the before/after slider in our 3D Smile Studio to watch a crooked arch align in real time.

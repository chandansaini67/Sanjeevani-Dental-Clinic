Dental Implants in Dimapur

A dental implant is a small titanium post placed in the jawbone that acts as an artificial root, topped with a crown that looks and chews like a natural tooth. At Sanjeevani Dental Clinic in Dimapur, an implant with crown costs ₹25,000–₹45,000 per tooth and can last decades with good care.

Implants are ideal when

A tooth was extracted or knocked out
You don't want to grind healthy teeth for a bridge
Dentures feel loose or uncomfortable
You want the longest-lasting replacement
The jawbone is healthy (we check first)
You're done waiting to smile confidently

What happens, step by step

  1. Assessment

    X-rays and examination confirm your bone is ready and plan the exact implant position.

  2. Implant placement

    The titanium post is placed under local anaesthesia — most patients are surprised how comfortable it is.

  3. Healing period

    Over 3–6 months the bone bonds with the implant (osseointegration), creating a rock-solid root.

  4. Crown fitting

    A custom crown matched to your natural teeth is fixed on top. Eat, speak and smile normally.

About our doctors

Common questions

How long do implants last?

With good hygiene and regular check-ups, implants routinely last 15–25 years and often a lifetime. They are the most durable tooth replacement dentistry has.

Am I too old for an implant?

Age is rarely the issue — bone health matters more. We've placed implants for patients in their seventies. The assessment tells us clearly if you're a candidate.

Is the placement painful?

It's done under local anaesthesia and most patients report less discomfort than an extraction. Mild soreness for a few days is managed with ordinary painkillers.

Why do prices vary from ₹25,000 to ₹45,000?

Implant brand, the crown material you choose, and whether extra procedures (like bone support) are needed. You get an exact written quote after assessment — before anything begins.

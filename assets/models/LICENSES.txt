3D model: assets/models/smile-arch.glb

Derived from BodyParts3D anatomical tooth meshes (14 upper permanent teeth).
Source: BodyParts3D / Anatomography, The Database Center for Life Science (DBCLS),
via the mirror https://github.com/Kevin-Mattheus-Moerman/BodyParts3D

Processing (offline): individual tooth STLs were welded, normalized with one shared
matrix (preserving the anatomical arch), decimated (meshopt), and combined into one
meshopt-compressed GLB with per-tooth named nodes (t11–t27). The BodyParts3D gingiva
mesh was not used; the gum ridge on the site is generated procedurally.

CC Attribution-Share Alike 2.1 Japan.
